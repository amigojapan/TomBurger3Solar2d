How to play

this is a hamburger shop simulation game.

you need to cook the hamburgers and french fries that sekino
asks you to make within the time limit while avoiding the poop
and the mouse.

how to make hamburgers and cheese burgers:


get the buns form the (toaster)
put the buns on the (dressing table)

you must follow these instructions in order:
put (ketchup) on the buns
put ketchup back on dressing table
put (mustard) on the buns
put mustard back on dressing table
put (pickles) on buns

get patty from (patty fridge on the botton right)
put it on the (griddle)
when cooked flip before burning
take patty to buns, put on buns
put spatula back in it’s place where you got it from(bottom of griddle)
(for cheese burger, put (cheese) )
for regular burger get (red wrapper) and wrap the burger
for cheese burger get (yellow wrapper) and wrap the cheese burger

take burgers to the (orders tray )

how to make french fries



go to the (fries fridge
over the griddle) as many times as necessary
go to (the scale ), measure the number of fries you want to make
repeat until the number of fries you want to make.
take the measuring bowl to (the frier ),
put measuring cup back on scale.
wait until they are ready, take the fries to (the salter )
the number of fries will be put in (the waiting bin )

how to clean up mouse poop


take (the broom )and clean up (the poop )
when done put the broom back in it’s
place by touching (the trash can ).

check the remaining orders by looking at (the orders spinndle).

you may get rid of some items you are holding by throwing them in the trash can
some items you must put back where yo got them form,
like the spatula, the fryer basket,
the mesuring bowl and the ketchup and mustard.
